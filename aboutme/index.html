About Me

Sister Herrera

Hello WDD 130!

My name is Sister Brittany Herrera. I am a recent graduate of BYU-Idaho. I have been a home engineer for 10 years. I previously received a Psychology degree from The University of North Texas before I began my family adventures. I worked for approximately 7 years in the software engineering field as a marketing program lead which led me to purse a degree in Computer Information Technology.

I am now an on-line instructor teaching WDD 130 Web Fundamentals at BYU-Idaho. In the Church I am currently the Primary Activity-days leader as well as a Compassionate Service leader for my ward. I live in the Austin, Texas area.

My husband and I are both converts to the church and have 4 daughters between the ages of 8 and 16.

My hobbies are watching my girls play volleyball and having family time. I also love to do landscaping, home projects, photography and genealogy.
